Hi there! Thanks for taking the time to stop by and check out my portfolio! My name is Theo Grant, and I am a software engineer that has worked primarily in the games industry. I am an alumni of the University of Illinois where I earned my bachelors in educational technology. I later earned my masters degree in game engineering at the University of Utah. While much of my experience is based in game projects, I have worked on a variety of projects including educational research, full-stack web development, and medical software.
